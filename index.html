Ingredients

  • > 2 Carrots
  • > 1 Potato
  • > White cabbage to taste
  • > 1 Stalk of celery
  • > 4 Grains of onion
  • > 3 Clove of garlic
  • > Pepper
  • > Shrimp paste
  • > 1 Small tomato
  • > 2 Meatballs
  • > Nutmeg
  • > 10 Peas
  • > 1 tsp Salt
  • > 1000ml of Water
  • > 100ml of Oil

Steps

    1

  • First, peel the skin of the carrots, garlic, onions and potato, cut the leaves that are on the tomatoes. Then cut into small sizes carrots, white cabbage,peas, potatoes and also 2 meatballs.

    3

  • Third, add a little pepper, 1 teaspoon of salt, a little nutmeg, 2 cloves of garlic, 4 grains of onion ,and a little shrimp paste into the pestle, then grind until smooth.

    5

  • Fifth, put all the vegetables and spices that have been mashed into the saucepan except the celery.Then wait about 16 minutes.

    7

  • Seventh, After 16 minutes, put the celery and garlic that has been fried into the saucepan ,and then turn off the stove.

    2

  • Second, wash all the vegetables that have been cut. After washing, cut the tomatoes into 8 parts.

    4

  • Fourth, prepare 1 saucepan, then fill it with 1000ml of water and heat it until it boils.

    6

  • Sixth, turn on the stove that has been filled with 100 ml of oil on low heat. Then thinly sliced ​​1 clove of garlic,and fry the chopped garlic until brown.

    8

  • And finally the soup is ready to eat.
